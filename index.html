About Me

Hi! My name is Kevin Hsieh (Shay). I'm a student with the University of Washington Full Stack Bootcamp. I stumbled upon this opportunity a few weeks after moving across the country from Maryland to Washington. Before being accepted into this program, I had zero coding experience. In fact, in undergrad, I studied to become a physical therapist.

Prior to moving across the country and beginning my journey in the world of tech, I was an entrepreneur. My first business revolved around High-Performance tennis coaching. And I loved it. I got paid to play and teach the sport that I love. As someone who was born and raised in the tennis community in Maryland, it felt great to be able to give back to the community.

Outside of all that, my 2020 has been crazy, even without the move. As you can see in the picture provided, beginning January, I was fortunate enough to travel to Asia (Indonesia and Japan) and come back before Covid had spread.
